Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfecion, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables or meats

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

    • Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese,diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

    • Instructions

    1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are
      well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or mil for a flufflier texture.
    2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil
    3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and blubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the
      pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface
    4. Add fillings (optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly
      runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one falf of the omelette
    5. Fold and serve: As te omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it
      over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate
    6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
